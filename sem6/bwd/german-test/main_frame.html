Narsee Monjee Institute of Management Studies (NMIMS) is one of India's top private universities offering quality education at the graduate and postgraduate levels. Established in 1981 by Shri Vile Parle Kelavani Mandal (SVKM), NMIMS was accorded 'Deemed to be University' status in 2003.

Launched as a management institute, NMIMS over the years, ventured successfully in other streams of education namely Engineering, Science, Pharmacy, Commerce, Arts and Design. The university currently operates out of eight campuses -- Mumbai, Bengaluru, Shirpur, Hyderabad, Indore, Navi Mumbai, Dhule and Chandigarh. NMIMS is accredited by NAAC with 3.59 CGPA and Grade A+ in the third cycle and UGC and MHRD have awarded NMIMS university category "1st" in the graded autonomy.

The university has been ranked 9th amongst the top MBA institute in India in Business Today rankings 2019. Admission to NMIMS is done through entrance exams. For admission to UG courses, the university conducts NPAT while for postgraduate management courses, candidates have to appear for NMAT by GMAC.
